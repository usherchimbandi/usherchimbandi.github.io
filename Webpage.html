There is no limit to admission; the institution is able to train people of all levels of education, elderly and young, we offer basic education skills in terms of reading and writing for people who have never been in a basic school environment, basic computer literacy for those people who are in a working environment and cannot be able to operate the computer optimally.

Duration of courses (Full time or part-time)

We offer full-time and part-time classes to cover people with busy schedules of life and work; we work extensively, including weekends and after hours. We also do online classes for those who are unable to physically attend classes.
